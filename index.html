Charcod.es

What is this‽

Charcod.es is a search-engine for Unicode - the specification that encodes (most) human alphabets, quotation marks, many mathematical symbols, and much, much more.

Try out searching for arrows, chess pieces, emojis or enter a unicode char/number to get more information about it.
